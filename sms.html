Opt-Out & Help

You may withdraw consent and stop receiving messages at any time by replying to any Buffalo Trace Bottle Drop SMS Alert with one of the following commands:

STOP Unsubscribe immediately. You will receive one final confirmation message and no further alerts.
HELP Receive a support message with contact information and unsubscribe instructions.
INFO Receive program information including message frequency and this page URL.

After texting STOP, you may re-subscribe at any time by re-submitting the form above. Your previous subscription will not be automatically restored.

  • Your phone number is used only to send the alerts you consented to receive.
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your phone number with third parties for marketing purposes.

We retain your phone number while you are an active subscriber. Upon receipt of a STOP command or an unsubscribe request by other means, your number is removed from the active send list.
